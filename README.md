# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

This **Sales Performance Dashboard** provides a clear and interactive view of key business metrics, helping users analyze purchasing behavior, promotion effectiveness, and customer satisfaction. It is designed to support data-driven decision-making through simple and insightful visualizations.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🔢 KPIs (Key Performance Indicators)

* **Purchase Amount**: ₹9,634.79
* **Promotion Usage**: 30,080
* **Satisfaction Score**: 5.01

These KPIs give a quick snapshot of overall sales performance and customer engagement.

---

## 📈 Visualizations

### 1. Sales by Education (Bar Chart)

* Displays sales distribution based on customer education levels:

  * Bachelor
  * College
  * High School
  * Masters
* Helps identify which education group contributes most to sales.

### 2. Sales by Product (Line Chart)

* Shows purchase trends across product categories:

  * Beauty
  * Books
  * Clothing
  * Electronics
  * Food
  * Health
  * Home
* Useful for understanding top-performing product categories and trends.
